About Me

Why DALI?

I found DALI through some upperclassmen friends
who inspired me to pursue Computer Science with their
works.
I found that, though I have only spent such
a short time learning CS,
I love being hands on and creating with a team.
Through my experience with coding,
I've fully begun to comprehend why
so many people say that coding is rewarding;
when I am finally able to get my code to work,
the satisfaction is comparable to that of when
I finish a long hike up a 14er.
And when I can't get my code to work,
I find that, unlike other school assignments,
I can sit and look through my code for hours without
feeling extremely bored.
Coding is something like magic...
And DALI is the perfect place to grow
in my coding skills and be apart of a team
that can cohesively create something together.
Though I am still perfecting Python in CS1 right now
and am still teaching myself HTML, Javascript and CSS,
I feel that I would learn quickly and enthusiastically
in DALI.



Thanks for visiting!

-Ashley
